Fullstack Developer

Jakarta, Jabodetabek Full Time Rp 16.000.000 - Rp 18.000.000

Job description

A Full-Stack developer is a professional responsible for working on both front-end and back-end development processes. They design, develop, and maintain fully-fledged and functioning platforms with databases or servers. These servers do not need other third-party applications to build an entire system from scratch.

Responsibility

  • Proven experience as a Application Developer
  • Attention to detail
  • Understanding of Agile methodologies
  • Help or collaborate with Technical Writer to document throughout the software development life cycle (SDLC)

Qualifications

  • 2 Years of Experience as Fullstack Developer
  • Experience and Familiar with PHP, Java or Mobile and Windows Form Application .Net Knowledge of the .NET languages (e.g. C#, Visual Basic .NET) and HTML5/CSS3
  • Experience and Familiarity with the Any Framework such as CI/laravel, ASP.NET framework, SQL Server. Mysql and design/architectural patterns (e.g. Model-View-Controller (MVC)), Etc
  • Improve existing software
  • Diligent, caring and good time management
