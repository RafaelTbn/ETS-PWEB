Digital Marketing Manager

Surabaya, Jawa Timur Full Time Rp 18.000.000 - Rp

Job description

Digital marketing managers are responsible for implementing, overseeing, and managing digital marketing strategies that advance an organization's mission by reaching a broad audience and attaining greater exposure.

Responsibility

  • Planning, compiling, executing and reporting all digital marketing activities, including SEO/SEM, database marketing, email, social media, and advertising.
  • Analyze the achievement of digital marketing activities according to ROI and KPI
  • Planning social media content (Facebook, Instagram, Website, and Youtube)
  • Develop a partnership campaign with key opinion leaders and influencers on social media

Qualifications

  • 6 Years of Experience in Digital Marketing
  • Good in digital campaign concept and planning skills
  • 2 Years of Experience as Team Leader
  • Good in analytic tools, Google Display Network, SEO/SEM and Social Media management
  • Good in analytical and planning skill
